About Eli's Pastry Café:


We are a small local coffee shop located in New Bedford, Massachusetts. Both family owned and family operated, and to top it all off it was family renovated too. It may not look like it but this cozy little coffee shop started off as a barber shop, and was like that for many years. That is until we gathered up our savings and renovated this little spot. We heard that the previous business was closing down due to lack of business. We saw it as an opportunity and took it, creating the current coffee shop you see here today. And so, we hope you'll stop by and have a cup of coffee or even just stop by for a little chat.
